About me



I have been interested in electonics and computers for sometime. My dad worked at Atari in shipping when I was young.
In high school, I took drafting and later attended vocational school for credits in electronic test and assembly.
In my twenties, I worked at Atari as well, but with my background I was working on the assembly line.
After a few years, I started working in the troubleshooting area, fixing and making repairs as needed for QA approval.
Afterward, I worked installing x-ray machines throughout Califorina, mostly for chiropractors and veterinarians.
I left the electronic field and worked at San Jose Unified School District as a custodian.
It was at this time I had my first PC and found America Online for the first time. I found a Star Trek chat room
and just chatted and role played at all hours. I met my best friend there and though we didn't like each other to at first,
we have now been married over 19 years. We have two wonderful redhead little girls who are 12 and almost 8.
We like to tease them and say if it wasn't for Star Trek, they wouldn't be here.
My wife is a teacher. We made a promise to each other years ago when we first got married that once she had a steady job,
I would quit my job and go after my dreams.
So a few months ago with my wife's blessing, I quit my job of almost 20 years to pursue my passions.

Now it's my turn!
